Cricket ( Men )

Rules

  1. Tournament will be conducted for men only.

  2. No. of players shall not be more than 15

  3. Tournament will be conducted for league cum knock out basis.

  4. 15 overs matches will be conducted during league stages, 20 overs matches from semis.

  5. Name of the ball to be used for the tournament: SS / SG / BDM

  6. White cricket costume is compulsory for playing cricket matches. Matches will be conducted in red color ball

  7. Latest BCCI- Board of Control of Cricket in India rules will be applicable.

  8. Cricket championship trophies will be awarded for the top 3 positions.

  9. Best player award will be awarded.
