Chess ( Men & Women )

Rules

  1. Tournament will be conducted for men and women separately.

  2. No. of players not more than 4 from each IIITs.

  3. Tournament will be conducted for Swiss method.

  4. The Host will provide the chess mat, coin and watch.

  5. Latest ICF - India Chess Federation rules will be applicable.

  6. Chess championship trophies will be awarded for the top 3 positions.
