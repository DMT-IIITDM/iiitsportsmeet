Athletics ( Men & Women )

  1. 2 participants will be allowed for an event, for relay only one team consisting of 4 participants are allowed to participate.

  2. An athlete shall be allowed to participate in maximum of 3 events excluding relay races.

  3. Heats will be conducted for 100m, 200m, 400m, 4x100 m relay and 4x400 m relay only.

  4. Curve start/finals will be conducted for all other track events

  5. Field events: 3 trials will be conducted for selecting the top 8 positions, and further 3 trials will be conducted for deciding the 1, 2 and 3 place among the top 8 athletes

  6. Medals and certificate will be awarded for first 3 positions only.

  7. Individual championship for men and women will be awarded separately.

  8. Athletics championship for men and women separately also will be awarded for top 3 position from the total medal Talley.
