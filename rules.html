General Rules and Regulations


  • All bonafide full time students of IIITs are eligible to participate in this 1st Inter IIIT Sports Meet 2016.

  • The maximum strength allowed for each IIIT Sports contingent strength is 70. Out of 70 students, the maximum number of girls allowed is 30. The maximum number of boys allowed is 50 (but the total no of participation should not exceed 70)

  • Registration fee shall be Rs 2000 per participating student for boarding and lodging.

  • Refundable medical caution deposit will be Rs 25,000 (for handling immediate medical emergency if any).

  • All participants should carry their institute ID card during the meet.

  • All participants should attend March Past.

  • Participating IIITs should carry their institute flag.

  • Teams should report to the competition venue 15 minutes before the commencement of the match.

  • All teams should wear the proper sports attire.

  • Trophies, Medals and Certificates will be awarded to top 3 positions.

  • Men & Women Championship trophies will be awarded to top 3 positions in the total medal tally.

  • For further details mail us to interiiitsports@iiitdm.ac.in
