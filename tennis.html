Tennis ( Men & Women )

Rules

  1. Tournament will be conducted for men and women separately.

  2. No. of players (Men) - not more than 4
    No. of players (Women) - not more than 4.

  3. Tournament will be conducted for league cum knock out basis.

  4. Matches will be conducted for best of 11 games in league stages and best of 3 sets will be played from Semis.

  5. Order of play for men:
    1st Singles, 2nd Singles, 1st Doubles, 1st Reverse Singles and 2nd Reverse Singles.

  6. Order of play for women:
    1st Singles, Doubles and 2nd Singles.

  7. Name of the ball to be used for the tournament: Wilson / Head/ Bablot.

  8. Latest AITA - All India Tennis Association rules will be applicable.

  9. Tennis championship (men and women) trophies will be awarded for the top 3 positions.
