Badminton ( Men & Women )

  1. Tournament will be conducted for men and women separately.

  2. No. of players (Men) - not more than 5. No. of players (Women) - not more than 3.

  3. Tournament will be conducted for league cum knock out basis.

  4. All matches will be conducted for best of 3 sets of 21 points.

  5. Order of play for men: 1st Singles, 2nd Singles, 1st Doubles, 3rd Singles and 2nd Doubles. Order of play for women: 1st Singles, Doubles and 2nd Singles.

  6. Name of the cork to be used for the tournament: Yonex / Victor / Li-Ning / RSL

  7. All players must play with non marking badminton shoes only.

  8. Latest BFI- Badminton Federation of India rules will be applicable.

  9. Badminton championship (men and women) trophies will be awarded for the top 3 positions.
